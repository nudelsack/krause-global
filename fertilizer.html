Urea 46% – Prilled/Granular Fertilizer

Urea 46% is a highly concentrated nitrogen fertilizer in prilled or granular form, primarily used in agriculture. It is a white, free-flowing granule containing 46% nitrogen, suitable for a wide range of crops. The fertilizer is free from harmful substances and is manufactured internationally according to standards such as GOST 2081-92.

Urea 46%

High concentration nitrogen fertilizer with 46% N content. Available as prills or granules for various applications.

46% Nitrogen GOST 2081-92

Automotive Urea – AdBlue®/DEF

Automotive Urea, also known as AdBlue® or DEF, is an aqueous urea solution with 32.5% high-purity urea in deionized water. It is used in SCR systems (Selective Catalytic Reduction) of diesel vehicles to reduce NOx emissions. The product meets the ISO 22241 standard and is colorless, non-toxic and odorless.

AdBlue® / DEF

Aqueous urea solution (32.5%) for SCR systems to reduce NOx emissions in diesel vehicles.

Euro 6/VI Compliant ISO 22241

Benefits

NOx Reduction

Reduces NOx emissions by up to 90% and helps meet Euro 6/VI standards.

Environmentally Friendly

Non-flammable, non-polluting.

Better Performance

Improves engine performance and fuel efficiency.

Extended Lifespan

Extends the lifespan of the exhaust system.

Easy Handling

Easy handling and refilling.

Applications

Diesel Vehicles

For diesel vehicles with SCR technology (e.g. trucks, buses, cars).

Industrial

For stationary diesel engines and generators.

Refilling

Directly into the vehicle's AdBlue tank.
