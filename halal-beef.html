About Our Beef Supplier

A Polish company with over 30 years of experience and one of Europe's largest beef producers. With a well-developed logistics and transportation network, the company exports its products to over 60 countries on three continents – Europe, Africa, and Asia.

Production Capacity

Modern production and breeding facilities can process up to 1,400 cattle daily, flexibly fulfilling large orders.

Quality Standards

HACCP, IFS, and BRC certified. Specializes in Halal slaughtering, addressing markets with diverse cultural and religious requirements.

Product Range

From high-quality fresh meat and portioned meat halves to frozen and packaged products, plus ready-made products for gastronomy and the food industry.

Thanks to seamless process control – from breeding through slaughtering and cutting to packaging – the supplier ensures consistently high meat quality and product parameters at world-class level in every market. As a reliable B2B partner, the company maintains long-term relationships with its customers and supports them in handling large orders through on-time logistics and individually tailored cooperation conditions.

Half Carcasses (Forequarters & Hindquarters)

Our supplier offers both forequarters (e.g., 5-rib forequarter with flank from heifer, bull, or cow) and hindquarters as part of their fresh and frozen beef range. Available in chilled or frozen form, portioned for export.

Export Weights

Forequarters: approx. 80–120 kg
Hindquarters: approx. 100–150 kg
Standard for international trade, customizable to customer specifications.

Halal Slaughter

Manual slaughter in compliance with SFDA and GSO standards. Halal certification by Polish Institute of Halal, recognized by SFDA (Saudi Food and Drug Authority) and SASO/GSO, including GSO 2055-1 for halal food chain requirements. Facilities are certified and registered for export to Saudi Arabia.

Freezing & Packaging

Freezing: Deep frozen at -18°C or below using modern technology to ensure quality and safety during transport.
Packaging: Hygienic, export-compliant materials (e.g., vacuum-sealed or bulk cartons) suitable for long-distance container transport.

Half carcasses are available in both chilled and frozen forms, portioned for international export and can be adapted to meet specific customer requirements.
