Your Partner for Global Commodities Trading

Founded with the goal of connecting global markets, Krause Global Resources & Trade is a leading player in international trading of commodities and goods. Our focus is on reliable procurement of large volumes for an exclusive network of institutional partners.

With decades of experience and deep understanding of global supply chains, we have established ourselves as a reliable partner for companies that prioritize highest quality, transparency, and sustainability.

Our Mission

We strive for clarity in all transactions, truth in our commitments, and reliability in fulfillment. Sustainable trade is our guiding principle – we prioritize environmentally responsible practices and long-term relationships.

Our goal is to be the bridge between producers and institutional buyers, while maintaining the highest ethical standards and creating economic value for all stakeholders.
