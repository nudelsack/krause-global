Frequently Asked Questions

How fast can you deliver?

Delivery times vary depending on product, quantity, and destination. Typically, we can deliver within 4-8 weeks. We offer express options for urgent requests.

What are your minimum order quantities?

We specialize in large volumes for institutional buyers. Minimum quantities vary by product but typically start with container or railcar loads.

Do you provide quality certificates?

Yes, all our deliveries come with complete quality certificates from independent laboratories. We meet all relevant international standards.

How can I become a partner?
