Certification LBMA Standard

Krause Global only supplies gold bars certified according to the LBMA standard (London Bullion Market Association). This certification guarantees that the manufacturer complies with the “OECD Guidance for Responsible Mineral Supply Chains”, in particular with regard to human rights, high-risk countries and money laundering prevention. Gold bars with this standard meet the highest requirements worldwide and are tradable without restrictions. The LBMA standard, also known as “Good Delivery”, makes the gold bars acceptable to banks and traders worldwide without the need for further are required.
